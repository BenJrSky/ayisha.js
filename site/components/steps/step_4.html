Step 4

📋 Different Types of Inputs

Ayisha.js works with all HTML input types. Let's try them all!

📝 The Code:

<input @model="name" placeholder="Your name">
<input @model="age" type="number" placeholder="Your age">
<input @model="agreed" type="checkbox"> I agree to terms
<select @model="city">
  <option value="">Choose city</option>
  <option value="paris">Paris</option>
  <option value="tokyo">Tokyo</option>
  <option value="newyork">New York</option>
</select>

🎮 Try it:

📊 Your Profile:

Name: {{profileName || '(not entered)'}}
Age: {{profileAge || '(not entered)'}}
Agreed: {{profileAgreed ? '✅ Yes' : '❌ No'}}
City: {{profileCity || '(not selected)'}}
🎯 What you learned:
  • @model works with all input types
  • Numbers, checkboxes, selects all bind automatically
  • Use || to provide fallback values
  • Ternary operators work great for conditions
