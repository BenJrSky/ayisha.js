Step 2

👋 Your First Interactive Element

Let's create your first interactive element. We'll ask for your name and greet you!

📝 The Code:

<input @model="userName" placeholder="What's your name?">
<p>Hello {{userName}}! 👋</p>

🎮 Try it:

Hello {{userName || 'there'}}! 👋

🎯 What you learned:
  • @model="variableName" creates two-way data binding
  • {{variableName}} displays the variable's value
  • When you type, the display updates automatically!
