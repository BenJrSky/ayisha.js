Step 8

🔄 Tabs and Switches

Create tab interfaces and switch between different views easily!

📝 The Code:

<div class="tabs">
  <button @click="activeTab = 'home'" @class="{'active': activeTab === 'home'}">Home</button>
  <button @click="activeTab = 'about'" @class="{'active': activeTab === 'about'}">About</button>
  <button @click="activeTab = 'contact'" @class="{'active': activeTab === 'contact'}">Contact</button>
</div>

<div @if="activeTab === 'home'">🏠 Welcome to the home page!</div>
<div @if="activeTab === 'about'">ℹ️ About us page content...</div>
<div @if="activeTab === 'contact'">📞 Contact us at...</div>

🎮 Try it:

activeTab = 'home';
