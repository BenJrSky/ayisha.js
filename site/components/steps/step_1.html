Step 1

🛠️ Setup - Your First Ayisha.js Page

Let's start with the absolute basics. All you need is one script tag!

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My First Ayisha.js App</title>
</head>
<body>
  <script src="ayisha.js"></script>
</body>
</html>
✅ That's it! No build tools, no complex setup. Ayisha.js automatically scans your page and makes it reactive.
